Who We Are

We are a collective of modified car enthusiasts. Our vision is to create an established community of like-minded enthusiasts in the local area, with aims to expand further afield within the UK and eventually abroad.

LA aims to develop a presence across the entire modified car spectrum, from chilled-out underground meets, to huge, bursting shows, mental track days, drift events, and everything in between.

Local to Global

Drawing inspiration from the North American car scene, we want to be the go-to name for UK petrolheads. Throughout the show season, you can find us at almost every major event in the calendar. Look out for our distinctive stall, and our insane rep cars; you can't miss it!

However, we don't plan on stopping there. Our mission is to create a global brand which unites car enthusiasts all over the world. You can help us on our journey by attending our events, connecting with us on social media, and spreading the word.
